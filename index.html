General Engagement Flow

By contracting MATR Tech, you gain on-demand access to difference makers willing to go the extra mile and develop truly custom solutions to your non-trivial problems.

  • Analysis

    1-6 Weeks

    We do not charge for this phase. During this initial period, we meet with you repeatedly, determine whether we have the capability to take on your project, and begin sketching out our approach and general timeline.

  • Iterative Design & Prototyping

    4-32 Weeks

    After completing the initial analysis phase, we commit to your project with a statement of work and enter several sub-phases of prototyping. Each iteration can be restricted to its own statement of work to minimize financial overhead and risk. Weekly or bi-weekly update meetings are scheduled.

  • Implementation

    4-12 Weeks

    Final changes are integrated into the product resulting from the previous phase. Comprehensive and organized documentation is finalized and compiled for the resulting system. An overall testing plan is signed off by both Client and MATR Tech.

  • Testing & Integration

    2-6 Weeks

    The developed system is rigorously tested in the live environment and documentation is cross referenced to ensure completion and understanding by in-house employees and leadership. System is checked-off by in-house leadership as error free and finished to certify completion of initial development and deployment.

  • Continuous Support

    System Lifetime

    Contingent statement of work is signed and implemented stating that MATR Tech is accessible on short-notice in the case that any system errors appear and require immediate attention.

Simulated Environment Construction

Initiated & Completed September 2022

A realistic warehouse environment was developed from drawings and photographs for the testing and validation of Clients’ systems without need for travel and transportation to end user location. The environment contained dynamically reconfigurable fiducial markers on storage racks for use with Client’s imaging system.

  • Client: Bespoke Robot Manufacturer
  • Category: Industrial Robotics

Point Cloud Localization

Initiated August 2022, Completed September 2022

Infrared depth cameras were integrated with Client hardware and software to augment system function in lowlight and restricted conditions. Resulting depth images were converted to point clouds and processed to extract features that were fused with system odometry and other sensory outputs to facilitate localization.

  • Client: Bespoke Robot Manufacturer
  • Category: Industrial Robotics

Skid-Steer Loader Automation

Initiated October 2022, Completed December 2022

A custom hardware and software platform was developed to allow the Client to control a skid-steer loader manually via a handheld transmitter and run autonomous sequences locally onboard the loader as well as on a remote server. Failsafe control logic was integrated throughout subsystems to ensure operational safety.

  • Client: Small Agricultural Facility
  • Category: Industrial Robotics

Custom Media Player Implementation & Integration

Initiated October 2022, Completed January 2023

Previously used devices had experienced premature and frequent field failure resulting in significant cost to Client and were not readily available due to supply chain strain. A custom triggerable media player was developed to mitigate the aforementioned issues and provide Client with ability to make modifications in-house.

  • Client: Emergency Services Systems Manufacturer
  • Category: Embedded Systems

Control System Analysis & Redesign

Initiated December 2022, Completed February 2023

Client control systems were commercial-of-the-shelf holiday prop and home automation controllers interfacing with an unmapped and untraceable network of automotive relays. Points of failure and potential improvements were identified through the existing system and a redesigned system maximizing robustness and simplicity was proposed.

  • Client: Emergency Services Systems Manufacturer
  • Category: Embedded Systems
